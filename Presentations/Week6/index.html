The wireframe I came up with to solve Larry's website issues is organized and user friendly. I included the nav bar right in the header. As well, I included a small image underneath with body text beside it. This makes users eyes flow to the body text because of the images appealing to them. Finally, I included social media links to Facebook, Twitter and Instagram to direct customers.
